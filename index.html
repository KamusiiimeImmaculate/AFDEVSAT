About AfDevSat

AfDevSat is a pan-African satellite initiative launched to leverage space technology for monitoring climate change, managing natural resources, and fostering regional development. The project brings together African countries under a shared mission: to develop and operate a small satellite for environmental monitoring and sustainable development.

Led by the Egyptian Space Agency (EGSA), and supported by national space institutions across the continent, AfDevSat empowers African engineers and scientists to co-design, build, and utilize satellite systems tailored to the region’s unique opportunities.
