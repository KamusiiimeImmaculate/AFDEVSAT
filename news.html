Uganda Hosts First AfDevSat Workshop

In May 2025, Uganda hosted the inaugural African Development Satellite (AfDevSat) workshop with participants from 20 countries. The event focused on mission planning, payload design, and fostering regional collaboration in space technology for sustainable development.
